A new Chapter

Dear Valued Guests and Friends of L'Appart,

Since its inception, L'Appart has been a culinary jewel in the heart of New York City, beloved for its intimate ambiance and exquisite dining experiences. Today, we are thrilled to share an evolution of this cherished space.

L'Appart is transforming into an exclusive private event venue, still nestled within the vibrant heart of Le District.

This transformation allows us to offer the unique charm and intimate setting of a private dining room for your next celebration, corporate gathering, or special occasion.

What to Expect?

  • Intimate and Exclusive Setting: The familiar cozy ambiance of L'Appart, perfect for private parties and gatherings.

We understand that change can be bittersweet, but we are enthusiastic about this new chapter and the opportunity to serve you in a more personalized and intimate setting.

Book Your Event:

Embrace the opportunity to create your own unforgettable moments in the transformed L'Appart space. Our team is ready to assist you in planning your perfect event. Connect with our Event Manager here to begin your journey.

We are deeply grateful for your continued support and love for L'Appart.

With heartfelt thanks,

The L'Appart Team at Le District
